Studio Art Pages
What will the Art Pages Beta Be?

The Art Pages Beta will be a fully functional version of the Art Pages platform where artists can build, update and host their websites. The beta version will be launched first to a limited number of artists to test functionality and help build new tools before the full launch takes place. During this period websites will be fully functional and viewable, but the platform will be available by invitation only. We want as many talented and driven studio artists as possible to participate in our beta group, so if you find yourself in need of a new website or interested in supporting the project we encourage you to sign up.

What Kind of Emails Will I Get?

Very few emails will be sent out to those who sign up. The only planned email is the one that will contain your login code and invitation when Art Pages is approaching it's beta launch or occaisionally if we have new resources available for artists that you may find useful. And of course you can remove yourself from this email list at any time.

Why Sign Up?

Space on the Art Pages Beta will be limited. Signing up here will allow you to build and host a free fully-functional professional studio website using the Art Pages platform as soon as the beta version launches (planned for 2019). During the beta period Art Pages will have a limited number of users and will be accessable by invitation only, although their websites will of course be fully functional and viewable on any device.
